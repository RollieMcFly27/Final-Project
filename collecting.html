Collecting Guide

Every journey begins somewhere, where will yours begin?

So, you've decided to take on this journey of collecting. Maybe you're doing it for the money or to have a collection of something, or maybe you're doing it because you have fond memories or you wish to satisfy your inner child.

Regardless of the reason, here's the beginner's tips you'll need before you begin. (NOTE: These are tips, not the law. As a collector, you must find your own path! Happy hunting!)

Tips and Tricks

Avoid Big Selling Sites
Websites such as eBay and Amazon are great overall, but if you're serious about collecting, you will want to avoid these two. eBay has a large amount of Chinese sellers selling knockoff games and consoles. Amazon doesn't have it as bad as eBay, but it's still a gamble and more often than not, you're paying more for the game than you would have otherwise.
Avoid Big Gaming Stores
Stores like GameStop were once your one-way ticket to getting games you couldn't get anywhere else. But in the recent years, these stores began to suffer under the economy and were forced to either sell other items to make ends meet or they raised the prices or they did even worse: both.
Always Double-Check the Price
No matter where you are or where you're buying from, when you buy a game, it's always good to check online for the average price. There's no specific place you need to go to for these prices, just simply check how much it usually goes for. It also helps to have an expert who knows the average prices well enough.
Find Your Local Game Shop
Look around for local game shops in your city and check their average prices, there's always going to be a few that follow in GameStop's footsteps in terms of pricing, but then there's honest shops that treat their customers fairly and price the items at market value or even lower. When you find a shop like this, become a regular! You never know what good will come from it!
Hunt for Garage Sales
Sometimes, your biggest scores happen in the most unlikely of places. If you find a garage or even a yard sale, make a stop to look around, you might just find something valuable within it. And it's important to keep some bit of cash on hand for these occasions as they may happen when you least expect it.
Always Check for Authenicity
Due to various questions surrounding this tip, this tip was given an entirely new section to answer any and all questions regarding this. There's many fakes and reproduction copies out there in the world and if you're a purist looking for the real deal, this page is for you.
